# Product

## Register

product

## Users

University program teams use this app to compare curriculum coverage, student readiness, and employer demand while planning interventions for skills gaps.

## Product Purpose

CareerSync helps universities understand where programs align with current career-market signals and decide which skills, actions, and employer collaborations should be prioritized.

## Brand Personality

Clear, analytical, practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid pitch-deck layouts, decorative dashboard clutter, and marketing-heavy copy that slows down staff who are trying to scan data and act.

## Design Principles

- Put decision-critical signals near the relevant table or workflow.
- Keep product surfaces dense enough for comparison without feeling cramped.
- Use color primarily for status, priority, selection, and data interpretation.
- Make recommended actions feel operational and immediately usable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use strong contrast, keyboard-reachable controls, clear focus states, and responsive layouts that keep tables usable on smaller screens.
