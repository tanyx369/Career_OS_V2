2. Problem

The education-to-employment gap is hard to see until it is too late.

Students may graduate without knowing whether their courses, skills, and projects match real job requirements. Universities may not detect fast enough when curriculum is drifting away from industry demand.

University education -> student readiness -> employer expectations -> career outcomes. Today, those signals are not intelligently connected.

Students

Do not know their skill gaps until applying for internships or jobs.

Universities

May miss emerging requirements in AI tools, analytics workflows, SQL, BI, and communication.

Employers

Cannot judge readiness from degree name or resume keywords alone.

3. Why Now

Industry skill demand is changing faster than traditional curriculum review cycles.

AI, automation, analytics tools, dashboards, and digital business workflows are reshaping entry-level roles. Static programme reviews cannot keep pace with real job descriptions.

SQL Power BI Excel automation Dashboard storytelling Stakeholder communication AI productivity tools Statistics Python basics Data interpretation
4. Solution

CareerSync AI compares courses, job demand, and student evidence in one alignment layer.

The system answers two critical questions: Is this university programme preparing students for the jobs they want? Is this student ready for this role, and what should they learn next?

Inputs

Course modules, learning outcomes, job descriptions, student skills, projects, certificates, and experience.

AI Analysis

Skill extraction, categorisation, matching, gap analysis, scoring, and explanation generation.

Outputs

Alignment score, missing skills, readiness profile, roadmap, project suggestions, and curriculum insights.

7. AI Features

Stage 1 can use LLM reasoning plus simple scoring, without heavy ML.

Skill Extraction

Extract skills from JDs and course descriptions.

Categorisation

Group tools, technical, business, AI, and soft skills.

Skill Matching

Compare required skills against covered skills.

Gap Analysis

Identify missing or weakly evidenced requirements.

Readiness Scoring

Weighted scores by importance and evidence quality.

Roadmaps

Recommend what to learn next and in what order.

Project Ideas

Suggest practical portfolio work that proves readiness.

Explanations

Explain why each gap matters in simple language.
